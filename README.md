# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date: May 10, 2026**

Apex Golf Lab ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

## Information We Collect

### Account Information

When you sign in with Apple, we receive your name and email address (or a private relay address if you choose to hide your email). We use this to create your in-app profile.

### Profile Information

You choose a username and avatar displayed on leaderboards and to friends. This information is stored in Apple's iCloud (CloudKit) and is visible to other users.

### Golf Data

Shot data, session history, club information, drill results, handicap rounds, and training statistics are stored locally on your device using Apple’s SwiftData framework. This data is not transmitted to external servers.

### Leaderboard & Social Data

When you complete drills or add friends, your username, avatar, rank, skill scores, and friend connections are stored in Apple’s iCloud public database (CloudKit) to enable leaderboards and social features.

### Location Data

With your permission, the app accesses your location to display weather conditions and wind data for your golf course. Location data is not stored or shared.

### Microphone & Speech

With your permission, the app uses your microphone and speech recognition during Live Training sessions to allow voice-logging of shot data. Audio is processed on-device and is not recorded or transmitted.

## How We Use Your Information

* To provide and personalize the app experience
* To display leaderboards and enable social features (friends, challenges)
* To calculate your handicap index and track training progress
* To show local weather conditions on the course

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties. We do not use third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. All cloud data is stored through Apple’s CloudKit infrastructure.

## Data Storage & Security

Local data is stored on your device using Apple’s SwiftData framework. Cloud data (leaderboards, profiles, friends) is stored in Apple’s iCloud using CloudKit, which is protected by Apple’s security infrastructure. We do not operate independent servers.

## Your Choices

* **Location:** You can revoke location access in Settings > Privacy > Location Services.
* **Microphone:** You can revoke microphone access in Settings > Privacy > Microphone.
* **Account:** You can sign out from within the app’s Settings. Your local data remains on your device.
* **Data Export:** You can export all your session and club data from the app’s Settings.
* **Deletion:** Uninstalling the app removes all local data. To request deletion of cloud data, contact us at the email below.

## Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
